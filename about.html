About me: 

Hi, I am a dedicated enthusiast of the indie game development scene, with a profound passion for artistic expression and graphic design. Through this platform, I am thrilled to share my ongoing creative endeavors with you.

Currently, I am in the development of two distinct projects. The first, "Retro World," is a single-player first-person shooter that is a testament to my commitment to capturing the essence of classic gaming in a contemporary setting. The second project, a prototype sword game, is currently in its prototype stages and it is designed to look like a game that is being played on a PlayStation 1 and Nintendo 64 console.

Although I have yet to publish any of my creations. However, I invite you to join me on this exciting journey, where I will share my progress and insights into the development process. Your support and feedback are invaluable as I strive to bring my creative visions to life. Thank you for being a part of this journey with me.
